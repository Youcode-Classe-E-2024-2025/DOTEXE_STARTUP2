What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods: credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For additional options, please contact our customer service.

What is the delivery time for my order?

Delivery times vary depending on your location. Generally, orders are delivered within 3 to 7 business days. You will receive a tracking number once your order has shipped.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

Yes, within 24 hours of purchase. After this period, the order will be in preparation and cannot be modified. Contact our customer service as soon as possible for any requests.

What should I do if I receive a damaged or defective product?

We apologize for the inconvenience! Please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order, and we will arrange a replacement or refund according to your preference.

How can I return a product?

Returns are accepted within 30 days of receipt. Products must be in their original condition and packaging. Contact our customer service to obtain a return label and the necessary instructions.
